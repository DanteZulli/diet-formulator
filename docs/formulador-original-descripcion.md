# Original Formulator Description

Source: https://medvetpreventiva.com/page/formulador-de-dietas

## About the Formulador MVP

The Formulador MVP is an online application designed for veterinarians to create balanced homemade diets in a practical, organized, and precise way.

It features a simple and intuitive interface for formulating individualized nutritional plans based on metabolic body weight requirements, following NRC standards. Some nutritional values and relationships have been adjusted according to the clinical criteria of veterinarian Trinidad Achterberg Barros.

## What can you do with the formulator?

### Centralize all patient information

Store in one place:

- Clinical data
- Nutritional plans
- Checkups
- Weight progression
- Body condition photos

### Create balanced nutritional plans

Formulate natural diets adapted to each patient's individual needs, considering:

- Nutritional requirements
- Pathologies
- Dietary preferences
- Specific nutritional management goals

### Work with a wide food and supplement database

Access a customizable database with 450+ foods and supplements, organized by categories.

Includes:

- Supplements commonly used in Argentina
- Products recommended by the author
- Manual or automatic food/supplement addition from the USDA database
- Multiple options to adapt plans for each patient

### Export nutritional plans to PDF

Easily print or send nutritional plans to clients in PDF format.

## Why work with an online application?

### Your data always protected

All information is stored securely and only you can access it.

### Compatible with any device

Use the formulator from computer, tablet, or smartphone, regardless of the operating system or device age, as long as it has an internet connection.

### Automatic and constant updates

The system is continuously updated with improvements and new features, no installations or manual interventions required.

## What is the formulator

A web application for veterinarians specializing in natural nutrition.

- Enables creation of complete nutritional plans: raw, cooked, or mixed
- Based on NRC guidelines, using requirements calculated from metabolic body weight
- Stores patient information with body condition photos, nutritional plans, checkups, and more
