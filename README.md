# Diet Formulator

Veterinary nutritional diet formulator for dogs and cats.

## Description

Web application for veterinarians to create personalized nutritional plans for canine and feline patients. Calculates nutritional requirements based on the patient's metabolic body weight according to NRC (National Research Council) standards.

The system was inspired by the work of veterinarian Trinidad Achterberg Barros and her Formulador MVP. This project is an independent, open-source reimplementation with no commercial relationship to the original product.

### Goal

To provide the veterinary community with a free, professional, evidence-based tool for:

- Creating patient profiles (dogs and cats, all life stages)
- Calculating energy and nutritional requirements
- Formulating balanced homemade diets (raw, cooked, mixed)
- Visualizing nutrient deficiencies and excesses in real time
- Exporting nutritional plans to PDF

## Planned Features (MVP)

### Profile Creator

- Patient data registration (name, species, age, weight, breed, body condition)
- Support for: adult dogs and cats, puppies/kittens, pregnant/lactating dogs/cats
- Automatic energy requirement calculation based on activity level
- Macronutrient target configuration (protein, fat, carbohydrates, fiber)
- Ideal weight and current weight management
- Photo registration (face, top view, side view)

### Diet Creator

- Patient profile selection
- Food addition with quantity and calculation type
- Real-time diet visualization
- Nutrient table with 5 columns: Nutrient/Value, Target, Total, %DM, %Target
- Sections: General, Protein, Lipids, Carbohydrates, Minerals, Vitamins
- Color indicators: black (insufficient), green (adequate), red (excess)
- PDF export with calorie and dry matter summary

### Food Management

- Database of 450+ foods and supplements
- Import from USDA FoodData Central (CSV/JSON, updated April/October)
- Manual food creation with complete nutritional information
- Categories: animal, supplement, cooked starch, other plants, recipe
